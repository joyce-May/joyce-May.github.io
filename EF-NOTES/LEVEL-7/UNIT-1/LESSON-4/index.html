撰写有关未来生活的文章

1. 词汇—有关未来的词汇

I—video

Todd: Hey, Joan! How was the conference?

Joan: Fascinating. I saw a very different idea of the future. And I think it’ll be good for business.

Todd: How do you mean?

Joan: You know how so many people are pessimistic about the future?

Todd: Yeah. It can be a little scary sometimes.

Joan: Well, all of the speakers at the conference were optimistic. For them, the future’s incredible.

Todd: That’s great, I suppose. But is it realistic?

Joan: I think so. The speakers talked about how technology is improving the lives of people in the developing world. Cellphones have brought better communication, and prople are getting computers.

Todd: And computers need software.

Joan: Exactly. Africa will be a huge market for us soom.

Todd: It will.

Joan: I also saw a demonstration of some technology that would be great for us.

Todd: Really? What was it?

Joan: The latest teleconferencing technology. We did a call with Tokyo, and it was like they were in the room with us.

Todd: It would be wonderful to have that here.

Joan: Yeah. We could have more effective meetings with Sunset Computers in New York.

Todd: Or with Lindstrom in Sweden. Fewer long business trips.

Joan: Exactly what I was tinking.

II—连线，将单词与其定义或具有相同含义的单词进行配对

effective => works well

demonstrate => show and explain

teleconference => video meeting

developing => improving

market => a place to sell things

III—选择正确的词

Buying new teleconferencing equipment isn’t 【 realistic lifelike developing 】 . We don’t have the money right now.

Our market share in Europe and North America is good. Now we need to sell more in the 【 realistic lifelike developing 】 world.

Can you 【 teleconference develop demonstrate 】 how the new software works?

Soon, all businesses will communicate by 【 teleconference market world 】. No more plane trips!

China is one of our most important 【 demonstrations worlds markets 】 .

Teleconferencing technology allows us to have very 【 effective developing market 】 meetings.

IV—听音频、录音频

New markets are opening in the developing world. [正在发展中国家打开新的市场。]

Africa will be a huge market for us in the future. [未来，非洲将成为我们的巨大市场。]

Mark demonstrated some new, effective technology. [马克演示了一些效果很不错的新技术。]

For a teleconference, you need a large-screen TV. [如果想举行电话会议，你需要一个大屏幕电视机。]

The conference on technology was excellent. [探讨科技的会议开得很好。]

2. 语法—量词more、less和fewer

I—notes

量词 more、less 和 fewer

1>. 用 more 加名词说明某件事物数量或程度的增加。

In the future, more people will work from home.

We’ll do more business with Africa.

2>. 用 fewer 加可数名词复数说明某件事物数量的减少。可数名词是可以与数字一起使用的名词。例如，我们可以说 one hour 或 two hours。可数名词有复数形式，如 cats 和 houses。

People will work fewer hours.

In the future, fewer people will travel on business.

3>. 用 less 加不可数名词说明某件事物数量的减少。不可数名词是不可以与数字一起使用的名词。例如，我们无法对 gasoline. 计数。液体与抽象名词，如 work，通常都不可数。

Soon, we’ll use less gasoline.

We have less work this year.

II—选择正确的词

We need 【 fewer less more 】 engineers. We don’t have enough right now.

In the future, people will spend 【 more fewer less 】 time at the office because they’ll work a lot from home.

The population of the world is increasing, so we need to produce 【 more less fewer 】 food.

Unless gasoline prices decrease, we’ll have to take 【 less fewer more 】 plane trips.

We’re unhappy because our boss gave us 【 more fewer less 】 time for creative thinking this year.

With robots doing more of our work, we’ll need 【 more fewer less 】 humans.

3. 表达—谈论人们的观点

I—连线

They talked about the developing world.

They believe that technology will improve.

She has fascinating ideas for the future.

One person said that he was very worried.

Some people seem pessimistic.

II—听音频、录音频

Some people seem pessimistic about the future. 【有些人似乎对未来很悲观。】

He believes that all electricity can come from the sun. 【他相信所有的电能都能来自太阳。】

For them, the future’s incredible. 【对于他们来说，未来一片光明。】

The speakers talked about technology improving lives. 【发言者谈论了能够改善生活的科技。】

All of the speakers were very optimistic. 【所有的发言者都非常乐观。】

The speakers had fascinating ideas for the future. 【发言者对未来充满美好设想。】

4. 阅读—阅读有关未来的文章

I—阅读文章

下面是3个人的话术：

  1. The speakers at the Bright Ideas conference last year gave optimistic ideas about the future. I liked what Henrik Mayer said. He believes that technology will provide more health services to people who live far from hospitals. If a sick person has a mobile phone, the doctors will use teleconferencing technology to help sick people by phone. This seems realistic, and it will help people have fewer health problems in the future.

  2. I read a university report that said that developing countries, the US and Europe strongly disagree about pollution laws. The report said that developing countries will create more pollution than other countries because they have fewer laws about air and water quality. The report is very pessimistic, but I think it is also realistic. I believe that world leaders need to meet and discuss this, or we will have terrible environmental problems.

  3. A German CEO says that Africa will be a huge market for computer businesses. The CEO believes that African economies will grow by 100% by the year 2050. I am also optimistic about Africa, but it’s not realistic that the economy will grow 100% in such a short time. Africa needs fewer laws that hurt business before the economy will grow that quickly.

II—句子排序

A German CEO says that Africa will be a huge market for business management.

He is optimistic. He believes the African economy will grow by 100% by the year 2050.

I am also optimistic about the African economy, but it’s not realistic that the economy will grow 100% in such a short time.

Before the economy can grow that quickly, Africa needs fewer laws on doing business.

5. 最终任务—撰写有关未来的文章

写一段有关未来的话。你认为未来将发生什么？你持乐观的态度还是悲观的态度？

范文：

I’m generally very optimistic about the future. I believe that gasoline will be gone by the year 2100. I think that’s completely realistic. We will use the sun and wind for electricity, and most cars will be electric cars. A scientist at Sunlight Motors demonstrated an electric car that can go almost 250 kilometers between charges. Many people are buying electric cars now. I think that pollution will decrease a lot over the next 10 or 20 years. Life will be good in the future.
