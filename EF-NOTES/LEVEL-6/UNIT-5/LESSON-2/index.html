讨论kennel的解决办法

1. 词汇—更多商务词汇

I—video

Simon: So, where do we start?

Todd: Well, Joan wants ideas for cutting costs.

Simon: Umm. The first thing I can think of is delaying some projects.

Todd: Which ones?

Simon: Well, right now we’re planing to build a new database.

Todd: Oh, yes. That’s a huge project.

Simon: In my opinion, we don’t need it right now. We can wait six months.

Todd: How much will that save?

Simon: I believe we can save half a million dollars.

Todd: Half a million? That’s fantastic.

Simon: Yeah. We won’t have to hire new developers or a project manager.

Todd: Okay. What else?

Simon: We could delay the release of our new accounting software next year.

Todd: No. We can’t do that. I’ve already promised our best customers that it’s coming in about a year. How many of your people are leaving in the next six months?

Simon: Uh, two that I know of. You’re thinking of a hiring freeze, right?

Todd: Yes, I am. It’s better than layoffs.

Simon: Okay. I can lose one developer, but I’ll still need to hire one new person for the Kingston project.

Todd: Hmmm. That’s an important project. Let me think about it, okay?

II—听力资料

He was hired as a developer six months ago, just before the hiring freeze . He’s working on a database with his project manager to support new software . The software release is in six months. It’s huge. They’ve already spent more than half a million dollars. The company’s customers are very excited.

III—选出正确的词

We keep all the company information in a huge (database release manager) .

My project (customers manager release) does a great job for the team.

Do you know the date of the next (sales release software) ?

No computer can operate without (database release software) .

A smart company should listen to its (profits releases customers) .

We’ll need to hire six or seven new (delays databases developers) to complete the project.

IV—听音频、录音频

Half a million dollars is a lot of money.

When is the company’s next software release? (软件发布)

He does a great job as project manager.

We can save money with a hiring freeze. (停止招聘)

She works as a developer in a software company.

We keep all our information in a huge database.

2. 语法—think和believe引导的从句

I—notes

含有 ‘think’和’believe’的从句

1>. 使用动词 think 和 believe 表达观点。注意，观点位于 that 从句，紧随动词之后。

I think that Joan did a good job.

Simon believes that we can save half a million dollars.

2>. 单词 that 可以省略，意思不会有改变。

I think Joan did a good job.

Simon believes we can save half a million dollars.

3>. 回答是或否的时，用 so 替代 that 从句，以避免重复整个短语。

A: Do you think that Joan did a good job?

B: Yes, I think so.

C: No, I don’t think so.

II—听力材料

KAREN: So, John, what did you think of the meeting?

JOHN: Well, I think that the company has some problems.

KAREN: Because of the hiring freeze?

JOHN: Yes, and I don’t believe that the software project is going well.

KAREN: Yeah, and the release is in two months. Do you think that we can do it?

JOHN: I don’t think so .

KAREN: That’s not good. Our customers are waiting.

JOHN: Yes. The project manager needs to do more.

3. 表达—表达观点

I—notes

表达观点

1>. 表达观点有很多方法。比如说，你可以使用 in my opinion 和 it’s my feeling (that) 这样的表达。

-In my opinion, we could delay the software release.

-It’s his feeling that we need to cut costs.

2>. 你也可以使用 I think 和 I believe 。记住，这些从句里面 that 可用可不用。

-I don’t believe that we should lay off staff.

He thinks a hiring freeze is better than layoffs.

II—dialog(听力资料)

SARAH: In my opinion , we need to cut costs.

PAUL: Yes, but how?

SARAH: Well, I think that we could save half a million dollars with layoffs.

PAUL: No, I don’t believe that’s the best idea. It would hurt employee morale.

SARAH: So, what’s your idea?

PAUL: It’s my feeling that a hiring freeze is the best way.

SARAH: Well, maybe, but it will take longer to save money.

III—听音频、录音频

I don’t believe layoffs are the best idea.

He doesn’t think that the layoffs are the best idea.

In my opinion, we need to delay the release.

Her feeling is that we need to delay the release.

I think that we could save half a million dollars.

No, I don’t think we could save that much.

4. 表达—请求澄清和确认

I—notes

请求重复、澄清和确认

有时，当您进行讨论并发表意见时，可能需要重复，澄清或确认。

请求重复

I’m sorry, could you repeat that?

I’m sorry, but I didn’t catch that. (没听明白)

I’m sorry, would you mind repeating that?

请求澄清

-Do you mean that you think it’s a good idea?

-Are you saying we should stop the project?

请求确认

-So you think he should be fired?

-So, just to confirm, you believe that it’s too expensive?

你也可以重复对方说的话。

Half a million?

II—连线

So, just to confirm, …?

So you think that … ?

Would you mind repeating that?

Are you saying that … ?

I’m sorry, but I didn’t catch that.

Do you mean that … ?

III—听音频、录音频

I’m sorry, would you mind repeating that?

I’m sorry, could you repeat that?

So, just to confirm, you believe it’s too expensive?

So you think that he should be fired?

Are you saying we should stop the project?

I’m sorry, but I didn’t catch that.

Do you mean that you think it’s a good idea?
