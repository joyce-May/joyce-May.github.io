给予反馈

1. 词汇—描述员工的形容词

I—video

Peter’s words.

Leo’s my best friend.

We’ve been friends for years.

We went to the university together.

I graduated from the university, and he didn’t.

He’s worked ar a pet shop since last year.

​That’s one year of feeding animals, walking animals, cleaning animals.

In the past, he’s been a waiter, a gardener…

​I mean, there’s nothing wrong with that, but Leo’s really clever.

​He’s reliable, outgoing, honest. He could do anything he wants.

The problem is, he wants to do nothing.

He’s totally disorganized. He’s just not ambitious.

I’am the opposite.

I graduated from the university and been working hard for the last five years.

I am ambitious.

I have goals.

​Leo’s never had a good job.

He’s never tried to find one.

I don’t understand it.

I am gonna find him a real job.

II—notes

描述员工的形容词

1>. 以下是一些可用于描述员工的形容词

cleaver — stupid

outgoing — shy

2>. 注意，否定前缀un-常用于构成反义词

reliable — unreliable

ambitious — unambitious

3>. 否定前缀dis-不如un-常见，但是也用于构成一些重要的反义词

honest — dishonest

organized — disorganized

III—听音频，录音频

I’m outgoing - I love to talk with new people.

I lost my ring, and I didn’t tell her. I feel dishonest.

Leanne had a great idea. She’s so clever.

She knows where everything is. She’s very organized.

Yes, I’m ambitious - I want to be CEO.

Max is never late to work; he’s very reliable.

2. 语法—Fast、hard和good

I—notes

‘fast’, ‘hard’ 和 ‘good’用作形容词和副词

1>. 很多形容词在词后面加上-ly即可用作副词。

He’s clever.

He managed the project cleverly.

2>. fast, hard 和 good都是例外。 用fast作形容词放在名词前面。

She’s a fast learner.

3>. 用fast作为副词放在动词之后。注意它没有-ly后缀。

She learns fast.

4>. 用hard作为形容词放在一个名词的前面。

Bill is a hard worker.

5>. 用hard做副词放在动词后面。注意: 它没有-ly后缀。

Bill works hard.

6>. 用good作形容词放在名词前面。

He’s a good guy.

7>. 用well，不是good放在动词后作副词。

I work well with the team.

3. 语法—for和since表示持续时间

I—notes

用for和since表达持续

1>. 用for谈论一段时间

Dave met with us for an hour.

Laura will work in Munich for three years.

2>. 用since谈论强调从过去某一个时间点起至今的一段时间。

Lindsey and Carol have worked here since Wednesday.

He’s been our customer since last year.

注意: since通常与现在完成时使用。

4. 表达—谈论工作表现

I—notes

谈论员工表现

1>. 在评估员工表现时，经理可能找你了解一位同事或你的下属的信息。你肯呢个会听到以下问题。

How long has she been on you team?

In general, how’s he doing?

Can you give me some details?

2>. 经理在了解一位员工的优缺点后，可能会马上询问你他怎样才可能改进。注意提问和问答中could一次的使用。

A: How could she improve?

B: Well, she’s sometimes late to work. She could take an earlier train in the moring.

A: What could he do better?

B: Umm, he could pay more attention to deadlines.

3>. 使用表达working on it和getting better, 谈论正在进步的员工。

He’s often disorganized, but he’s working on it.

She’s sometimes a bit shy in meetings, but she’s getting better.

II—听音频、录音频

How is Jack doing, in general?

In general, he’s doing very well.

Can you give me some details?

Well, he works hard, and he works fast.

And how could he improve?

Let’s see. Sometimes he’s pretty shy.

Is that a big problem?

No, it’s getting better. We’re working on it.
