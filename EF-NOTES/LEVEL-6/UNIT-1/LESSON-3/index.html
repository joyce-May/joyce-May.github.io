主持会议

1. 词汇—会议词汇

I—video

Todd: Thanks for coming, guys. I konw that you’re really busy. Have you looked at the agenda?

Sally: Yup. Only three items?

Todd: Yes. New hires, office visits and a company update. We only have a short time, so let’s get started.

Sally: Okay.

Todd: First, new hires. Simon just hired a new chief engineer. She’s from China. Her name’s Amy Ling. Simon said that this is her first time in the US, so, um, please make her feel welcome. And, uh, Jessica in HR is leaving the company.

Harry: Really? Why?

Todd: It’s for personal reasons. Next: office visits. Joan told me that we have some important visitors in the office next week. And you know what that means.

Sally: We have to clean up our desks.

Todd: That’s right. Any questions before we continue?

Harry: Um, can we talk about the company update? That’’s what’s really important.

Todd: Yes. Harry. We’re getting to that.

II—notes

有关会议的有用词汇和短语

以下是一些回忆中经常出现的词汇：

Has everyone got the agenda?

There are three items today.

FIrst, we have several new hires.

Jane is leaving for personal reasons.

I’ll give an update on that later.

III—阅读会议纪要

WEEkLY MEETING - 4/12

Meeting started: 2 p.m.

Agenda items

  1. NEW HIRES

    New architect(Air rivera) hired Tuesday. Worked at BTFL Designs.

    Tony leaving. Moving outside the country! Party for Tony - Friday at 3 p.m.

  2. OFFICE VISITS

    Three visitors next week(from New York). Clean up desk.

  3. COMPANY UPDATES

    Continue with employee reviews in May.

Meeting ended: 2:15 p.m.

词组：

new hire

clean up

agenda item

IV—听音频、录音频

How many new hires were there last month?

There are four items on the agenda.

Let’s look at the meeting agenda.

He’s leaving the company for personal reasons.

She will give a company update in the meeting.

2. 语法—‘say’ 对比 ‘tell’

I—notes

使用 say 和 tell 引述他人说的话

1>. Say和tell意义相近

He said that she’s very nice but a little shy.

He told me that she’s very nice but a little shy.

2>. 你可能注意到，tell通常与me、you、或us等词搭配使用，而say无需搭配这些词

He said he would tell us the news next week.

3>. 如果希望语气更随便，在say或tell引导的句子中就不需要that

He said he would let us know.

He told me the news about the move.

4>.如果引述will或can之类的情态动词引导的句子，将情态动词变为过去时形式

John told us he would check.

Tandy said she could finish the work herself.

II—听音频、录音频

We have six new hires.

Lisa told me that we have six new hires.

My vacation is in two weeks.

Lias told me her vacation is in two weeks.

Tell John he should clean up his desk.

John, Lisa said that you should clean up your desk.

3. 表达—主持会议

I—notes

主持会议

可以分步安排会议，每一步骤都清楚明了。

  1. 问候和感谢参与者

    Welcome, everyone. Thanks for coming.

    Good morning. Glad you’re all here.

  2. 阐明议事日程

    Has everyone looked at the agenda?

    There are seven items on the agenda.

  3. 指明会议时间安排

    We don’t have much time.

    We have only 30 minutes.

  4. 使用顺序副词，一次进行各项议题

    First, office cleaning.

    Next, new hires.

    Now let’s talk about the Ramsey project.

  5. 确保参与者明白并提问

    Any questions before we continue.

II—dialog

Jan: Has everyone seen the agenda?

Roy: Yes. Two items, right? And we have only 10 minutes. Wow!

Jan: I know. So, let’s talk quickly. FIrst, office visits.

Penny: Yes. We have eight returning visitors from Japan next week.

Jan: Great. That should be no problem. We’ve met them before.

Roy: Yep. We know how to welcome them.

Jan: Any questions before we continue? Nope? Okay, let’s look at business updates.

III—听音频、录音频

Has everyone seen the agenda?

We don’t have much time, so let’s move quickly.

First, new hires. Dan’s leaving for personal reasons.

Li said that she can do his job while we hire someone.

Any questions before we continue?

Now let’s move to item two - company updates.
