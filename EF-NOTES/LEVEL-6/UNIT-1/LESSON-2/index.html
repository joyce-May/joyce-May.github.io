转达信息

1. 词汇—思想与情感

I—notes

描述我们的心理过程

1>. 在人群之间传递信息时，引用你的心理过程或你对他人心理过程的猜测是有用的

A: I was wondering when we’ll move to a new office.

B: As you konw, we need to move pretty soon.

A: We expect big changes in the next months.

B: Got it.

2>. 能够解释你和其他人的感受也很有用

A: People are working about job cuts.

B: It doesn’t surprise me.

II—video

Todd: So, I’m meeting with my team tomorrow, and I’m wondering what to say. Some people already know we’re buying another computer company.

Joan: It doesn’t surprise me. News travels fast.

Todd: Sally says that some employees are worried about losing their jobs.

Joan: Well, they shouldn’t worry. The deal is final, and it’ll be good gor the company. Sales will increase, and there will be no job cuts.

Todd: That’s good news. What can I tell Sally and Harry?

Joan: It’s best to be honest. They probably know a lot already. You can confirm that we’re buying Sunset Computers.

Todd: Okay.

Joan: If I were you, I’d tell them that we’re going to be a stronger company, and that they shouldn’t worry. The outlook’s good. We don’t expect any job cuts.

Todd: Great.

Joan: But, Todd, you have to tell them this information is confidential. It cannot leave the office.

Todd: Got it. Thanks, Joan.

III—听音频、录音频

OK, got it.

Can we expect better salaries?

I’m wondering what to say about the new office.

People are worrying about job cuts.

2. 语法— 引述他人说的话

I—notes

过去时和现在时的引述

1>. 当你引述某人说过的话，你用的引述动词，如say, 可以采用过去时。

Hey, boss, I was wondering…Brad said we’re moving to a new office.

2>. 你也可以采用现在时态引述。这突出强调该人说过的话与现在相关，即便说话在过去。

Some people are saying that it’s going to be really far from the center.

And Tanya says there’s no subway station nearby.

II—听音频、录音频

Everyone is saying there will be a new manager.

Benny said we’d move to a new office.

People said there wouldn’t be a new system.

Tanya says there will be job cuts.

3. 表达—询问和提出建议

I—notes

讲话许可

1>. 在公司知道什么能告诉别人，什么不能告诉别人，这又是很重要。类似这样的问句很有帮助

What can I tell people about the move?

Should I tell the guys?

2>. 相应的，你可能期待像这样的回答

A: What can I tell people about the move?

B: It’s best to be honest. It will happen very soon.

A: Should I tell the guys?

B: If I were you, I wouldn’t say anothing for now.

II—阅读课文（课文）

You: Steven, what can I say to the guys about the new computer system?

Boss: It’s best to be honest. We don’t have enough money for one.

You: Should I tell them now?

Boss: If I were you, I’d wait a few days.

You: OK. Lunch, later?

Boss: Sure.

III—听音频、录音频

What should I say to Donna?

You should confirm that the deal is final.

What can I tell the team?

If I were you, I’d say there will be some job cuts.

It’s best to be honest.

Should I tell our customers?

Yes, it’s always good to talk with customers.

Got it. Thanks.
