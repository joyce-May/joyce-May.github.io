填写处方

1. 词汇 — 医药词汇

I—video

Pharmacist: Next, please.

Pam: Hi. I’d like to fill a prescription.

Pharmacist: What’s your last name?

Pharmacist: James. My first name is Pam. Does my inscrance cover this?

Pharmacist: Yes, it does.

Pam: Great.

Pharmacist: I’ll be right back.

​ Are you allergic to any other medications?

Pam: Uh, no, I’m not.

Pharmacist: Okay. So this will help stop the diarrhea. The dosage is two tablets every six hours.

Pam: Okay.

Pharmacist: It’s better if you take it with some plain food, like rice or scrambled eggs. Nothing spicy.

Pam: I understand.

Pharmacist: And this is for the nausea and the vomiting. Take two capsules twice a day for three days. I hope you feel better.

Pam: Thank you.

Pharmacist: Next, please.

II—notes

医药词汇

1>. 形容获得prescription的动作，使用动词fell。当要去药店取药时使用动词pick up。

I’m going to the pharmacy.（药房）

He talked to the pharamacist.（药剂师）

She filled a prescription for her medication. (她按处方配了药)

2>. 药剂师可能询问你是否不能服用一些药物。注意形容词结构 + to。

Are you *allergic to * any medications? （过敏）

3>. 使用下列词汇谈论你的药物。谈论在家用药时，使用medicine搭配动词take。当你去药店时，使用更正式的词汇medication。

You forgot to take your medicine. (你忘记吃药了)

I’m here to pick up my medication. （我来取药的）

4>. 药物有各种不同形式。pill一词常用于指固定药物。Capsules和tablets两种最常见的药物形状。服药的动作使用动词take。

I have to take three pills a day. (我每天要吃三片药)

My medicine comes in tablets. （我的药是片状的）

She takes four capsules a day. （她每天吃四粒）

5>. 由于处方药有时由保险支付或有折扣，你可能会问药剂师你的医疗计划是否能支付某种药品。使用动词cover, 询问你要承担的费用。

Does my insurance cover this medication?

III—dialog(移词至正确的位置)

Doctor: I’m going to write you a prescription for some medication. The dosage is two tablets, twice a day.

Patient: Okay.

Doctor: You aren’t allergic to anything, are you?

Patient: No, I’m not.

Doctor: Good. And you’re not currently taking any other medication?

Patient: No.

Doctor: Great. Take it easy when you have an appetite again - nothing spicy for the first week, OK? By the way, I’m sorry to say your insurance won’t cover this. You’ll need to pay for it yourself.

IV—听音频、录音频

Are you currently taking any other medications?

She takes there pills for nausea and vomiting.

Hello. I’m here to pick up my medication.

The pharmacist asked if I’m allergic to any medication.

I need to go to the pharmacy to fill a prescription.

I hope my insurance will cover this.

2. 阅读 — 阅读处方标签

I—notes

阅读处方标签

1>. 药有不同形式-有如tablets或capsules的固体药物，或如你用teaspoons服用的如syrups的液体药物。你将在处方标签上找到你的施药信息。

Take four tablets, twice a day.

The medicine comes in capsules. Take two every day. (胶囊包装)

Take one teaspoon, after meals. （餐后服用一匙）

2>. 标签也会提醒你有关的side effects, 你服药时遇到的难题。这会包括drowsiness或loss of appetite.

The side effect of this medicine is that it may cause drowsiness.

Side effects may include loss of appetite.

3>. 标签上也许会告诉你药的用量-maximum dosage-和谁suitable for该药-成年人或小孩。

Maximum dosage: four capsules per day

Suitable for: adults and children over 12 years old

II—阅读材料

Flaxo
Max dosage: 2 teaspoons per day
Side effects : may cause loss of appetite
Suitable for: 12 years old +

Lemonboy
Take three capsules every 24 hours. May cause drowsiness . Suitable for all ages .

3. 表达 — 与药剂师对话

I—连词成句

Are you allergic to any medications?

Does my insurance cover this?

Are you currently taking any medications?

This dosage is two tablets every three hours.

I’d like to fill a prescription.

This is for the nausea and vomiting.

II—听音频、录音频

I’d like to fill a prescription. (开张处方药)

Does my insurance cover this?

I’m not currently taking any medication.

It’s better if you take it with some plain food. (清淡食物)

Take two capsules twice a day for three days.

I hope you feel better.
