谈论疾病或受伤

1. 语法 — 事故与受伤

I—video

Pam: Jake, what happeded?

Jake: I, uh, fell down some stairs.

Pam: What? How did you do that?

Jake: I don;t know. I just, uh … fell.

Simon: Really? Why don’t you tell her the whole story, Jake?

Jake: I don’t know what you’re talking about.

Pam: Come on. Tell me what happened.

Jake: Well…

Jake: I was walking down some stairs, and I wasn’t paying attention.

Simon: Actually, you were paying attention, but not to the stairs.

Pam: Okay. What does that mean?

Simon: Well…

Simon: He was paying attention to a very attractive young woman, who was walking up the stairs as he was walking down.

Jake: I think the stairs were wet, or something.

Simon: Anyway, he fell down the stairs and broke his leg.

Pam: Oh, Jake. That’s awful.

Jake: Yeah. I bruised three ribs and, un, sprained my right wrist.

Pam: Come over here.

Pam: Ohhh. When did this happen?

Jake: Uh, two days ago.

Pam: And how are you coping with crutches?

Jake: Crutched? No problem at all.

II—notes

事故和受伤

你可以通过解释发生的事情或结果来描述事故和受伤。

发生的事情

I slipped and cut my arm. (我脚下一滑，把胳膊划伤了)

I fell down and bruised my shoulder. (我摔倒了，把肩膀碰伤了)

结果

I have a bruise on my arm. (我碰伤了肩膀)

I have a sprained wrist. (我扭伤了手腕)

I have a broken leg. (我摔断了腿)

2. 语法 — 同步展开的行动

I—notes

While和when描述中断的动作

1>. 在描述事故时，通常可以包含2个动作，其中一个动作(was + verb + ing)被另一个动作中断。

I sprained my ankle while I was playing soccer. (扭伤了脚踝)

I was walking on an icy road when I slipped and broken my leg.

2>. 正如你可能已经注意到的那样，你可以使用while或when来引入当时正在发生的动作。

While I was crossing the road, a woman fell down.

When I was driving to work, I saw the accident.

3>. 你还可以用when来引入第二个动作。

A: What happened?

B: I was climbing the ladder when I slipped.

II—选词

I was walking home when I fell down.

I was cooking when I slipped and cut my arm.

While I was plain football, I sprained my ankle.

When I was crossing the road, a car hit me.

IV—听音频、录音频

I bruised my shoulder while I was cycling. (碰上了肩膀)

I have a vruise on my arm. (胳膊擦伤了)

I sprained my wrist and bruised my leg. (扭伤了手腕，擦伤了脚)

I broke my arm while I was playing volleyball.

I fell off a wall and broke my leg.

I was running for a bus when I sprained my ankle. (扭伤脚踝)

3. 表达 — 回答关于事故的问题

I—notes

询问事故

1>. 在向人询问他刚经历的一场事故时，以下是一些常见的问题。

What happened?

Oh, no! Were you OK?

And how are you feeling now?

2>. 以下是一些常见的回答，通常降低了事故的严重性。

A: Hey. How was your weekend?
B: Mmmm, it was OK. Not so good.

A: What happened?
B: Well, while I was cooking, I slipped and fell down. （脚下一滑摔倒了）

A: Oh, no! Were you OK?
B: Yes. I went to the hospital, but it wasn’t serious. (问题不大)

A: And how are you feeling now?
B: I’m OK. I have a bruise on my head, but I’ll be fine.

II—听音频、录音频

I went to the hospital.

I slipped and fell while I was playing soccer. (滑倒了)

It wasn’t serious. (问题不是很大)

I’m OK. I have a bruise on my leg.

Mmmm, it wasn’t great.
