解释问题

1. 词汇—个人问题

I—video

Sarah: I need a coffee.

Mark: You want a hot coffee, now? Sarah, it’s like 115 degrees outside.

Sarah: I know. I’m addicted.

Mark: Are you serious? You know, you really should quit drinking coffee.

Sarah: But I need it. Anyway, what’s your problem? It’s just a cup of coffee.

Mark: Sarah, when you drink coffee, you smoke.

Sarah: No, I don’t. Mark, I stopped smoking.

II—notes

个人问题

1>. 如果你无法放弃停止做某事，你就是对它addicted, 或你对它有了一种addiction，下面就是一些常见的沉溺。

He’s addicted to cigarettes.

She’s addicted to coffee.

They’re addicted to gambling. （赌鬼）

He’s addicted to alcohol. He’s an alcoholic. （酒鬼）

2>. 以下是一些其他常见的个人问题。

She has financial problems.

He has a weight problem. He’s overweight.

I have a problem sleeping. I have insomnia. （失眠症）

They have marital problems. (婚姻问题)

III—匹配句子

A: They fight a lot.
B: They have marital problems.

A: He doesn’t sleep at night.
B: He has insomnia.

A: They can’t pay their bills.
B: They have financial problems.

A: She smokes every 15 minutes.
B: She’s addicted to cigarettes.

A: He can’t stop drinking.
B: He’s an alcoholic.

A: She eats too much.
B: She’s overweight.

IV—听音频、录音频

She can’t stop. She’s addicted to cigarettes.

They’re always fighting. They have marital problems.

He has a weight problem. He’s very overweight.

She doesn’t sleep at night. She has insomnia.

He’s addicted to coffee. He drinks 10 cups a day!

They’re having some financial problems.

He’s addicted to chocolate. He can’t stop eating it.

2. 阅读—了解大意

I—阅读原文

Poor John. He has a lot of personal problems. His job is very stressful. At night he can’t stop thinking about his job, and he has terrible insomnia. He has marital problems because he drinks too much. And he’s overweight because he doesn’t exercise. John needs to make some changes.

3. 表达—谈论问题

I—notes

谈论问题

1>. 在确定一个问题后，你可能想详细加以解释。以下是一些帮助你进行解释的表达。

I have insomnia. The problem is that I can’t stop thinking about work.

She can’t quit smoking. One big problem is that her husband smokes, too.

2>. that可省略而不会造成意义的改变。

He is very overweight. The trouble is he eats too muck chocolate.

She can’t quit smoking. One big problem is her husband smokes, too.

II—听音频、录音频

She has a lot of personal problems.

I’m sorry to hear that.

She’s having financial problems.

The trouble is that she’s addicted to shopping.

Oh, no. That’s terrible.

Yeah. She can’t stop buying clothes.
